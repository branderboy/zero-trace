How It Works

Our Remediation Process

From your first call to final clearance, we handle everything so you don't have to. Call us 24/7 at (202) 803-6356. We typically respond within one to two hours.

01

Call or Request a Quote

Contact us by phone or submit our online form. We'll gather details about the situation and provide a free estimate.

02

On-Site Assessment

Our certified team arrives promptly to assess the scope of work, document the scene, and coordinate with insurance if applicable.

03

Cleanup & Decontamination

We perform full biohazard removal, disinfection, and odor elimination using hospital-grade products and OSHA-compliant protocols.
