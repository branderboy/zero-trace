About Us

The Company

The Experience and Expertise You Need!

ZeroTrace Biohazard is a trusted leader in biohazard remediation, specializing in crime scene cleanup, trauma response, and hazardous waste decontamination. We are committed to restoring safety, protecting communities, and ensuring thorough, discreet, and professional cleanup services.

With advanced decontamination techniques, OSHA-certified protocols, and a highly trained team, we handle bloodborne pathogens, infectious disease control, industrial accidents, hoarding cleanup, and hazardous material disposal. Our ZeroTrace Guarantee ensures that every site we clean is 100% decontaminated, safe, and compliant with health regulations.

What We Do

Cleaning & Disinfection

During the cleaning and remediation process, we provide for efficient and affordable disinfection solutions to proactively help safeguard against a broad-spectrum of bacteria, viruses, and mycotoxins, including H1N1 (Swine flu), MRSA, Norovirus, E. coli, Salmonella enterica, Staphylococcus aureus, Legionella pneumophilia, Aspergillus niger and many more.

Utilizing Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) registered and Health approved disinfectants products, we ensure your family, pets and co-workers will not suffer any effects from the cleaning products we use. Whether we're cleaning a complex crime scene or a simple home clutter situation, every effort will be made to disinfect all treatable areas.

How Our Company Works

We'll ask for specific information to help us to determine the type of work or service you need. If you're in need of biohazard remediation, depending on your location, we can typically respond within a few hours. Pick a Time

Complete our "Request a Quote" form and pick an ideal date for service. We'll reach out to you, collect information and let you know if that date works for our team. If not, we'll schedule a time that's mutually beneficial.

Prior to scheduling service, we'll discuss payment arrangements. If insurance is involved, we'll typically need your homeowner's insurance policy deductible up front. The remaining cost of service will be billed to insurance.
