What We Do

Cleaning & Disinfection

During the cleaning and remediation process, we provide for efficient and affordable disinfection solutions to proactively help safeguard against a broad-spectrum of bacteria, viruses, and mycotoxins, including H1N1 (Swine flu), MRSA, Norovirus, E. coli, Salmonella enterica, Staphylococcus aureus, Legionella pneumophilia, Aspergillus niger and many more.

Utilizing Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) registered and Health approved disinfectants products, we ensure your family, pets and co-workers will not suffer any effects from the cleaning products we use. Whether we're cleaning a complex crime scene or a simple home clutter situation, every effort will be made to disinfect all treatable areas.

Pick a Time
